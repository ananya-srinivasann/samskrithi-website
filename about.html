About Us
Samskrithi School of Dance



Guru

Smt. Soumya Rajaram

Soumya Rajaram is a well-known dancer and teacher in the Boston area. She started her training at the Kalakshetra Foundation, a premier institute for performing arts in India. She is a recipient of the senior merit scholarship for dance by the Govt. of India and was an empaneled artist with the state television. She performs regularly at dance festivals in the Boston area and India. After moving to Boston, she started the Samskrithi School of Dance where she teaches a well-thought-out curriculum that inspires her students to discover the joy of dance with a sense of purpose it can bring to their lives. She has been recognized by the Massachusetts Cultural Council as a Fellow finalist every year since 2016.

Samskrithi School of Dance
